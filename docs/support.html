Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create flashcards?

Tap the + button on the Flashcards tab, create a deck, then add cards with questions and answers. You can also use AI to generate cards automatically!

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Go to Profile → Premium, then tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Quobby → Cancel Subscription.

Is my data synced across devices?

Yes! When you create an account, your flashcards, habits, and progress sync automatically across all your devices.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.
