Everything you need to learn

Powerful study tools designed to help you remember more in less time.

Flashcards & Spaced Repetition

Master any subject with smart flashcards that adapt to your learning pace. Add text, images, voice recordings, and example sentences. Study with classic flip cards, multiple-choice quizzes, or typing practice.

Language Learning & Auto-Translation

Learn vocabulary in 21+ languages with AI-powered card generation. Enable auto-translate on any deck to get instant translations as you type. Hear correct pronunciations with built-in text-to-speech.

Generate flashcards with AI, choose vocabulary level (beginner, intermediate, advanced) and focus on specific topics with tags.

Community Decks

Browse, download, and share flashcard decks with learners worldwide. Rate and review community decks. Publish your own with full moderation and content filtering.

Document Scanner

Handwritten Notes

Create beautiful notes with pen, pencil, marker, and highlighter tools. Use templates, shapes, rulers, and themed backgrounds. Export as image or PDF.

Habit Tracker

Build consistent study habits with daily tracking, streaks, and calendar views. Set reminders and monitor your progress over time.

Focus Timer

Block distracting apps during study sessions with Pomodoro or Full Block mode. Track your timer on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island with Live Activity. Skip periods or end sessions without opening the app.

Gamification & Rewards
